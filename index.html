    Take Action

    Across the United States, the push to demand that the ultra-wealthy finally pay their fair share is gaining momentum.

    CALIFORNIA

    The 2026 Billionaire Tax Act is being proposed as a midterm ballot measure to levy a one-time 5% wealth tax on the state's billionaires, projected to raise over $100 billion in a 5-year period. Find out more HERE: https://billionairetaxnow.org

    NEW YORK

    Zohran Mamdani is championing a 2% increase in city tax on incomes over $1 million, increasing top corporate tax rates to 11.5%, and extensive estate tax reforms to fund essential social services solve New York City's budget crisis. Find out more HERE: https://www.nyc.gov/mayors-office/news/2026/02/mayor-mamdani-releases-balanced-fiscal-year-2027-preliminary-bud

    CONGRESS

    Senator Elizabeth Warren and Representatives Jayapal and Boyle have reintroduced the Ultra-Millionaire Tax Act in Congress. If passed, this bill would implement a 2% or 3% annual wealth tax on household and trusts valued over $50 million or $1 billion respectively. Find out more HERE: https://www.warren.senate.gov/newsroom/press-releases/warren-jayapal-boyle-45-lawmakers-renew-push-for-wealth-tax-on-ultra-millionaires-and-billionaires
